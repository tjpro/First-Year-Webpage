What types of companies hire engineers?

  1. Nvidia

    Nvidia is the world leader in visual computing. Since 1999 they have revolutionized the world of computing with the invention of the graphics processing unit or GPU. For the past two decades, Nvidia has pioneered this product, which transformed the PC from a tool of productivity into one for creativity and discovery.

    This company is dedicated to delivering it's customers with the best possible GPUs that will give the most realistic visual experience possible. Today, they are branching out of the GPU market and making thier way into the handheld device market as well as integrating visual computing systems in the automotive industry.

    Nvidia is based out of Santa Clara, CA with other jobs located worldwide. With the average starting salary for computer engineers hovering around $65,000, this company is very competetive. Some of their products include:
    • GeForce Gaming GPUs
    • Tesla Professional GPUs
    • The SHIELD portable gaming system
    • GPU drivers

    Some of the services they provide include:
    • Consulting to graphic design professionals
    • Cloud-based gaming
    • Visual computing solutions for:
      • High perfomance computing
      • Mobile Devices
      • Automobiles
      • Much much more!
  2. Google

    Google is the worlds leading search engine. This company uses very complex algoritems and coding sequences to create the amazing tool that it is today.

    Over the years, Google has branched out from being a search engine, to being one of the worlds largest companies. Today, they still are a search engine, but they also are taking over the areas of mobile devices, tablets, and GPS navigation.

    Google prides itself on the fact that they are a global company. They have employees from all over the world and this diverse group allows the google to have a really cultured feeling in the work environment. Additionally, the offices where you work are the best around. They are made to be stress-free, fun areas that allow employees to think and work outside of the box. With the starting salary for computer engineers somewhere in the region of $70,000, Google would be a great company for anyone to work at. Some of their products include:
    • Android Phones
    • Android Tablets
    • Their famed search engine
    • Google Maps

    Some of the services they provide include:
    • Obviously, their biggest is their search engine
    • Google Play store
    • Google Drive
