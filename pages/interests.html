Why I love college

Freedom
I can do what I want, when I want. I am on my own to make mistakes, succeed, and fail all depending on what I do and do not do. It can be intimidating at first but if you can treat this freedom with a healthy dose of respect, it will prepare you for adult life.
Learning
Learning in college is different than anything you have probably experienced before. You must take full responsabliltiy for everything in class and do your best to master the material. However, you begin to learn about things that you are interested in so it is worth while.
Oppertunities
College presents you with the oppertuinity to join many diverse groups. The groups on campus are always open to new people and love people excited in what the group stands for. From acting clubs, to running groups, to professional development societies, there is a club for everyone.
Social Life
Living in Forbes hall, I have met some of my best friends because we found common ground in what we were studying. As engineers, we work, learn, and succeed in groups. The guy and girls here are great. Additionally, all of the people that you meet in different groups that you join only expands your friend group.
The chance decide your future
All of the groups, classes, and oppertunities that you can take advantage of allows you get a real glimpse into the future, the career that you want, and the life you want to live. College presents you with the chance to maximize you
The campus
The University of Pittsburgh is a beautiful place to study and live. Pittsburgh offers such a fun, friendly, and diverse environment to experience. Downtown has an awesome culture district. The strip district is a fun place to explore. Finally, south side is great place to find food. Overall, there is a lot to do and more than enough to explore.

I found out that if you don't do your work you will fail but if you do your work, you will succeed.

Something I thought college would be like, before you got here and it turned out to be false:
I found out that professors are people too and they really do care about you if you are willing to work hard in their classes. I came in with the impression that they wouldn't care, which was false.
