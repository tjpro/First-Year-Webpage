Interesting Professional Society

IEEE: Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers

"The world's largest professional association for the advancement of technology"

The Insitute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, or IEEE, is the most well-known professional society for computer and electrical engineers. Their mission is "to foster technological innovation and exellence for the benefit of humanity." This is a global community, and the largest of it's kind.

  • Who are the members of this society?
    • The IEEE is composed of over 450,000 engineering professionals in more than 160 countries.
    • There are over 116,000 student members in this organization.
    • The IEEE community is also home to professionals in other fields such as chemists, physicists, and doctors.
  • Why does this association exist?
    • The IEEE publishes nearly a third of the world's technical literature in electrical engineering, computer science, and electronics.
    • IEEE journals are among the most highly cited in electrical and electronics engineering, telecommunications, and other technical fields.
    • Each year, IEEE sponsors over 1,300 conferences. Over 100,000 technical professionals attend each year.
